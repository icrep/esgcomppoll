Bridge Building Competition

A bridge building competition where groups will design, test, and build a wood bridge. Each build will be judged by its maximum weight, earthquake resistance, and aesthetics.

Rubber Band Airplane/Glider

Groups will design lightweight gliders with rubber-band power trains similar to the boats from last semester's competition. Gliders will be judged by distance traveled, flight path, and adherence to the ruleset.

Open Design Exhibition

No design constraints but time. Students can work by themselves, or in a group of any size of their choosing, to solve an engineering or design problem.

At the end of the semester, students will present their projects and go over their goals and design journeys. Students can showcase detailed designs/schematics, simulations, videos, or live showcases of what they built.
